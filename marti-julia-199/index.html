Collblanc and La Torrassa are authentic, quiet neighbourhoods in L'Hospitalet, right on the Barcelona border. With Santa Eulàlia metro (L1) just 80 metres from the door, you have a direct connection to the heart of Barcelona. Local markets, shops, neighbourhood feel and the whole city a step away.
